## How Halcon works

Halcon organizes your business around a clear order workflow. Each department interacts with orders only at the stage relevant to them, keeping the process clean and auditable.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sales registers the order">
    The Sales team logs a new customer order with fiscal data, delivery address, invoice number, and notes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Purchasing and warehouse prepare materials">
    Purchasing acquires any external materials. Warehouse prepares and reports shortages if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Route distributes and captures evidence">
    The Route team marks the order as dispatched, then uploads loading and delivery photos on arrival.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customer confirms delivery">
    The client can log in to view their order status and see the photographic delivery evidence.
  </Step>
</Steps>
